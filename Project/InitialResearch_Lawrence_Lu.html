Initial Research

Problem Statement

The rate and effects of reading for the youth is continuing to plunder as the age of technology reduces the attention spans and interest of people while poverty reduces the access that children have to books.

Need Statement

Education is the major solution to communities who are impoverished, however, because they don’t have the resources and funding to receive the schooling necessary for college, degrees, and higher education they continue to fall into the vicious cycle of poverty. Books and increases in reading would benefit these communities because they would not only boost the education of the youth, but they would also incentivise social cohesion that lacks so much in our day and age.
